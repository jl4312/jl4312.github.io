About Me:

Hi, I'm Joseph Lu, a 3D Generalist and Programmer. From a young age, I was captivated by the magic of animation, visual media, and games. Now, with a degree in art, game design, and programming from the Rochester Institute of Technology, I am able to combine my passions to create magical, interactive media for children.

I specialize in 3D modeling, texturing, and programming, and strive to create a variety of media, from mobile games to educational apps. You can see some examples of my work here.

What drives me is the opportunity to inspire future generations with my work. I'm always seeking new ways to improve myself and explore new opportunities. Whether it's through learning a new programming language or experimenting with a new art style, I'm constantly striving to grow as a professional.

I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate and bring more magic to the world.
