Summary

Galactic Clapback is a mobile bullet hell where the player must dodge and parry the enemy spaceships to achieve the highest scores. With each new high score, players can exchange in-game currency for unique ships with different abilities and play styles. Although Galactic Showdown is no longer available on Google Play, you can download and play the game on itch.io.
