Summary

Mobile bullet hell where the player must dodge and parry the enemy spaceships. The main objective is to get the highest scores which exchange into in-game currency allowing players to purchase more unique ships. The game was developed for Android but has been removed from Google Play. Now you can play and download the game here or on itch.io.
