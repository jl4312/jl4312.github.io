Summary

VRsus guARdian is an exciting and innovative game that takes advantage of both AR and VR technologies. In this cat-and-mouse chase game, two players face off against each other in a thrilling race to victory. One player uses an AR-enabled device, while the other uses a VR system.

The AR player's objective is to track down and shoot down the invading player, while the VR player must collect relics scattered throughout the world and escape before getting caught. The environment is procedurally generated using AR scanning and assets that are placed randomly, creating a unique experience each time the game is played.

This project was a great way for us to explore the capabilities of AR and VR technology. One of the challenges we faced was the unpredictable placement of assets due to the procedural generation producing an inconsistent environment. To address this, we created "collections" of assets that could spawn together to create a cohesive environment with some items randomly spawning around, giving players the sense of being immersed in a unique and engaging world while maintaining some sense of randomness.
