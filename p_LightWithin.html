Role Duration
Freelance Technical Artist June 2022 - Present

Summary

The Light Within is an inspiring mobile puzzle game that blends captivating gameplay with a profound message about mental health. Players embark on a journey with Erin, a young girl grappling with her mental health, as she navigates through intricate puzzles symbolizing different rooms within her mind. Each level is meticulously crafted to delve into her inner world. The game features a distinctive low-poly isometric art style.

As a freelance tech artist for Pomsky Games, I played a key role in developing the immersive 3D environment. Collaborating closely with concept artist, Pam Dibujos, and 3D Modeler, Sofia Ramos, I handled a wide range of responsibilities including modeling, texturing, and optimizing environment assets, creating materials, prototyping and designing puzzles, and managing visual development and particle effects.

We are proud to announce that The Light Within has won the Unity for Humanity Grant, recognizing its impactful narrative and innovative design. Click here to learn more about this inspiring and thought-provoking game.

Responsibilities
  • Environment Modeling and Texturing: Develop and refine environmental diorama by modeling 3D assets, adjusting designs, and applying textures based on concept art.
  • Animation: Animate environment elements and interactive puzzles to enhance dynamic gameplay experiences.
  • Optimization for Mobile: Streamline level designs and optimize assets to ensure smooth performance and efficient loading times on mobile devices.
  • Lighting: Configure and fine-tune lighting settings to achieve optimal light bakes, ensuring visually appealing environments.
  • Shader: Design and implement custom shaders using Shader Graph in Unity to achieve unique visual effects and enhance environmental aesthetics.
  • Particle Effects: Develop and integrate particle effects for levels, interactions, and visual feedback, adding depth and immersion to the game world.
  • Puzzle Prototyping and Design: Design and prototype puzzle mechanics, iterating on concepts to create engaging and challenging gameplay experiences.
  • Greyboxing: Construct greybox models of environments to establish spatial layouts and gameplay flow before finalizing detailed assets.
  • Post-Processing Effects: Apply and adjust post-processing effects to enhance the overall visual quality and atmosphere of the game environment.
