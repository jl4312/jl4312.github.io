Summary

The Light Within is an inspiring mobile puzzle game that combines engaging gameplay with an important message about mental health. Players follow the journey of Erin, a young girl who is struggling with her mental health and must navigate her way through challenging puzzles that represent different rooms in her mind. Each level is carefully hand-crafted to explore her inner world. The art style for the game is a low poly isometric game. As a freelance tech artist for Pomsky Games, I was responsible for creating the immersive 3D environment that players explore. Worked closely with our talented concept artist, Pam Dibujos, and 3D Modeler, Sofia Ramos, I modeled, textured, and optimized the environment assets, as well as animated and optimized the puzzle pieces. Our inspiring and thought-provoking game has been submitted to the Apple Arcade. Click here to learn more about this inspiring and thought-provoking game.
