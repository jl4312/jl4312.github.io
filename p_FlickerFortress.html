Summary

Flicker Fortress is a single-player 3D puzzle platformer where the player plays as a firefly named Flicker who can meld/fuse with electrical devices. These electrical devices unlock different mechanics in the game. Some key devices are a light that can make an object appear and disappear when shined upon, a cord that allows Flicker to transport from one location to the next, and much more. Players will need to use their wits and Flicker's unique ability to overcome challenging puzzles and obstacles laid through a tower with its powers and uncover the mystery of where Flicker's friends have gone.

Backstory

Once upon a time, numerous fireflies were twinkling around. As time went on, they all vanished. Now only one remains, Flicker. She must voyage through a dark, mysterious tower that holds the secret of her missing friends. Where she gathers what remains of her brethren inside their encasements. Can you help her on her journey to find all her friends?

A personal project, I worked on with a group of friends during our free time. We wanted to create a fun engaging 3D puzzle experience. It was started from a game we previously worked on during college, ChiaroScuro. We revisited some of the mechanics and wanted to explore further in developing it. You can play the game now on itch.io at https://aquilateam.itch.io/flickerfortress.
