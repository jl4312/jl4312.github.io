The Light Within - Unity, C#

The Light Within is a captivating mobile puzzle game that seamlessly blends engaging gameplay with a powerful message about mental health. The player is immersed in the journey of Erin, a young girl battling with her mental health, who must traverse through intricate puzzles representing the inner workings of her mind. Each level is meticulously hand-crafted to explore her emotional landscape. The game features a captivating low poly isometric art style. The Light Within, has been accepted onto the Apple Arcade platform, a testament to its inspiring and thought-provoking message about mental health.

Flicker Fortress - Unity, C#

Enter a captivating world as a firefly named Flicker with the unique ability to meld/fuse with electronic devices in a 3D puzzle platformer. Explore a mysterious tower to uncover the mystery of where Flicker's friends have gone. Use your powers to solve challenging puzzles and overcome obstacles such as enemies and traps. Fuse with devices like lights, cords, and more to unlock new mechanics and progress further. As the artist, programmer, and designer, I worked on this personal project with a group of friends during our free time. Play now on itch.io and help Flicker find her friends!

Galatic Clapback - Unity, C#

Galactic Clapback is an action-packed mobile game that immerses players in a fast-paced, bullet-filled world where they must use their parrying skills to survive enemy spaceships. With gameplay reminiscent of the popular "Cop vs Cars" title, "Parry Space" features a unique low-poly art style, inspired by "Mars Mars". As players collect points they can obtain new ships by purchasing through shop and lottery. Created with a team of three developers. Worked as an artist, a game designer, and a programmer. My responsibilities were creating all of the game's art assets, textures, custom shaders, and VFX. As well as designed and programmed a shop, lottery system, and player progression system. VRsus guARdian - Unity, C#

VRsus guARdian is a thrilling cat-and-mouse game where one player utilizes an AR-enabled device while the other plays using a VR system. The objective of the AR player is to safeguard their relics from the VR/PC player, who moves around the level using hook-shot. The game's environment is procedurally generated using AR scan technology. I was responsible for managing the asset pipeline, recruiting, onboarding, and leading team members to create game-ready assets that follow the creative director's vision. The project was completed over the course of a semester, and the team utilized tools like Trello and Slack for asset management and communication. Overall, VRsus guARdian offers an exciting and unique gaming experience that incorporates both AR and VR technology. Click here to learn more.
